Automatically collected information: Our apps may collect anonymous usage data (like crash reports or device types) to help us improve performance and stability. We do not collect personally identifiable information unless explicitly requested and provided by you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

Our applications may use third-party services (such as Google AdMob for advertising or Firebase for crash reporting). These third-party services may collect information used to identify you, such as your IP address or device identifier. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party providers for more details.

4. Children's Privacy

Some of our applications, such as DuoBaby, are designed for early childhood development. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, and our children's apps are designed to comply with applicable guidelines, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top.
